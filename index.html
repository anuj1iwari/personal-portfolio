About Me

Beyond the syntax and semicolons.

Building Solutions, One Story at a Time

I’ve always believed that great code is more than just logic, it’s about the narrative we create for the user. I am a Storyteller by heart, and I bring that perspective to every line of code I write. Whether I'm building an AI-powered brain like Prism AI or a peaceful corner of the internet like ZenSpace, I focus on the experience, the emotion, and the problem being solved.

My journey into web development was driven by a simple curiosity: "How can I make the digital world feel more human?" Today, I use the MERN stack and Next.js to turn that curiosity into reality. From healthcare innovation with Vitae AI to the curated vibes of Lofi Focus Room, I love taking abstract ideas and sculpting them into scalable, beautiful digital realities.
